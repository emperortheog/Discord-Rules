### 1. Follow Discord’s Terms & Guidelines
> All members must adhere to [Discord’s Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms) and [Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines). Any violation of these terms may lead to removal from the server.
### 2. Treat Everyone with Respect**
> Be kind and respectful toward others. Hate speech, harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior of any kind is strictly prohibited. This is a welcoming space for all, and everyone deserves to feel safe.
### **3. Keep It Safe for Work**
> This is an SFW (safe for work) server. Please avoid posting or discussing any NSFW, graphic, violent, or offensive content. Usernames, nicknames, and profile pictures must also be appropriate.
### **4. No Spamming or Disruption**
> Do not spam messages, emojis, links, or pings. Repetitive or off-topic messages that interrupt the flow of conversation can make the server unpleasant for others.
### **5. Stay on Topic in Channels**
> Each channel has a specific purpose. Please use them accordingly. Keep conversations relevant to the channel topic and read pinned messages or channel descriptions if you're unsure.
### **6. No Advertising or Self-Promotion**
> Do not promote your own content, social media, servers, or websites unless given permission by staff or in designated channels. Unapproved advertisements will be removed.
### **7. Respect Privacy**
> Do not share personal information—yours or anyone else’s—without clear permission. This includes private messages, photos, or any sensitive details.
### **8. Cooperate with the Staff Team**
> Our staff is here to help and maintain order. Please follow their directions and avoid arguing with them in public channels. If you have concerns, reach out privately in a respectful manner.
